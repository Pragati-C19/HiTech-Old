Roof Top Solar

A solar photovoltaic (PV) system mounted on a rooftop of a building is a mini-power requirements or feed into the grid. Although anyone can install a solar rooftop system, the size of the installation varies significantly depending on availability of space, amount of electricity consumed by the property and the ability or willingness of the owner to invest the capital required.
